## Product / Service Description

GemShield is a pure-play jewelry replacement insurance product focused on personal jewelry owned by individual consumers (not jewelers or retailers).

**Coverage Scope:**
- Jewelry types: Engagement rings, wedding rings, diamond rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings, watches, gemstones, and all other jewelry
- Peril coverage: All-risk policy covering loss, theft, damage, mysterious disappearance/unexplained loss, floods, earthquakes
- Geographic scope: Worldwide coverage while traveling
- Transit coverage: Jewelry in transit for inspection, repair, or sale
- New jewelry grace period: 30-day limited coverage for newly purchased jewelry until added to policy

**Coverage Limits:**
- Maximum per piece: $35,000
- Maximum per schedule (total): $100,000
- Minimum: No stated minimum per piece

**What is NOT Covered:**
- Manufacturer defects
- Intentional loss or damage
- Nuclear hazard
- War and war-related damage

**Claims Process:**
- Customer files claim by calling 1-866-207-9428 and their original jeweler
- Claims administered by Berkley Asset Protection
- Jewelry repaired or replaced through the customer's trusted local jeweler
- Replacement is like-for-like (brand-for-brand, not downgrade to cheaper alternative)

**Application & Underwriting:**
- Online quote: <1 minute (no personal info required)
- Application completion: <10 minutes
- Appraisal requirement: Required for each item; must be no more than 18 months old for items valued >$5,000. Items <$5,000 can be insured with sales receipt; appraisal required within 10 days of application

## Pricing Model

**Pricing Structure:** Percentage-of-value annual premium with flexible deductible tiers

**Publicly Available Price Points:**
- $5,000 engagement ring: $52–$63/year (depending on deductible)
- $10,000 diamond ring: ~$200/year (zero deductible option)
- General formula: 1–2% of jewelry's insurable value per year
- Minimum premium: $20

**Deductible Options:** Multiple tiers available (specific amounts not publicly stated, but described as flexible to fit budget). Higher deductible = lower premium.

**Pricing Transparency:** Mid-range transparency. Customers can get quotes online without providing personal information, but exact pricing is customized based on appraisal value and deductible selection. No enterprise pricing tiers found.
